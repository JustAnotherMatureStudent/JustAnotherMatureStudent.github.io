Ingredients

One red onion

Jar of easy garlic

Chilli powder

One Lime

Ground turmeric

Peanut butter

Ground black pepper

400ml can of coconut milk

Chicken stock cube

Cornflour

Consider adding some chopped sweet potato or diced carrot to provide additional nutrition and texture

Method

Simply add each of the following to the slow cooker

1 diced red onion

2 teaspoons of easy garlic or garlic puree

1 1/2 teaspoons of chilli powder - more if you like your dishes a little hotter

Grate the zest and squeeze the juice of one lime

1 chicken stock cube

1 teaspoon of ground turmeric

2 tablespoons of crunchy or smooth peanut butter

A pinch of black pepper

1kg of chicken breast or thigh

1 can of coconut milk

Give the mixture a good mix

Cook on low heat for five hours


Serving Suggestions

Using two forks, shred the chicken and give it a good stir after returning to the slow cooker

Now is the time to add some cornflour to thicken up the mixture if required

Serve with rice and steamed vegetables
