Ingredients

2 white onions

20g garlic

1kg of cubed pork fillet

1 1/2 teaspoons smoked paprika

2 tsp oregano

3 tbsp red wine vinegar

200g passata

1 pork stock cube

3 mixed peppers

Method

Simply add each of the following to the slow cooker

Diced onions

Garlic

Pork

Paprika

Oregano

Red wine vinegar

Passata

Stock cube

Cook on low for 5 hours - add diced peppers with an hour cooking time remaining

Serving Suggestion

Serve with any of pasta, rice or gnocchi. Crusty bread and a dollop of sour cream also works well
