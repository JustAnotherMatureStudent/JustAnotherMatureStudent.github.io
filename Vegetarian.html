Ingredients

2 tins of chopped tomatoes

1 tablespoon of chilli powder, more if you like it HOT

2 tablespoon ground cumin

1 tablespoon ground coriander

1 heaped tablespoon easy garlic

1 onion

1 large carrot

1 large bell pepper

150g of wheat berries

Pinch of salt and pepper

1 tin of black beans, rinsed

1 tin of kidney beans, rinsed

Method

Chop onion, carrot and bell pepper

Add everything to the slow cooker

Give it a good mix

Cook on low for five hours

Serving Suggestions

Seerve with boiled rice

Sliced avocado and sour cream can also be delicious additions to your meal
