Ingredients

2 tins of chopped tomatoes

1 tablespoon of chilli powder, more if you like it HOT

2 tablespoon ground cumin

1 tablespoon ground coriander

1 heaped tablespoon easy garlic

1 onion

1 large carrot

1 large bell pepper

150g of wheat berries

Pinch of salt and pepper

1 tin of black beans, rinsed

1 tin of kidney beans, rinsed

Method

Simply add each of the following to the slow cooker

Chopped onion, carrot and bell pepper

Cumin

Coriander

Garlic

Wheat berries

Pinch of salt and pepper

Black beans

Kidney beans

Give it a good mix

Cook on low for five hours

Serving Suggestions

Seerve with boiled rice

Sliced avocado and sour cream can also be delicious additions to this meal
