Ingredients

1 kilogram of cubed or stripped stewing beef

50ml of soy sauce

25ml of honey

150ml of water

30g garlic

30g ginger

6 spring onions

Method

Simply add each of the following to the slow cooker

Combine soy sauce, honey and water then pour over beef

Garlic and ginger

Stir

Cook on low for 5 hours

Serving Suggestions

Beef can be served with rice or noodles - both are an excellent accompaniment

Roughly chopped spring onions and sesame seeds, depending on how much garnish you want on top of your meal, are a delicious addition

Steamed broccoli, yorkshire puddings and mashed potato also work well
